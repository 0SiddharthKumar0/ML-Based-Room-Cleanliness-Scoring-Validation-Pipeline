ML-Based Room Cleanliness Scoring & Validation Pipeline

Roles:
- Housekeeping Staff
- Supervisor
- Hotel Admin

Workflow:
1. Staff cleans room.
2. Staff uploads room image evidence.
3. ML service analyzes images.
4. System generates cleanliness score and confidence score.
5. System decides:
   - PASS
   - REVIEW
   - REJECT
6. Supervisor reviews flagged rooms.
7. Final decision is stored.
8. Audit logs record all actions.
